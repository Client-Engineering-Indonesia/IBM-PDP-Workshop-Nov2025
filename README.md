# IBM-PDP-workshop

Indonesia’s Personal Data Protection (PDP) Law is being progressively implemented across industries, requiring companies to not only understand the regulation but also put effective governance and protection measures in place. As a trusted technology leader, IBM is committed to supporting organizations with best-in-class solutions that address these regulatory requirements from a technology and operational perspective.

In this interactive workshop, participants will gain both theoretical knowledge and practical, hands-on experience to better prepare their organizations for PDP compliance. The agenda is designed to mirror real-world roles and responsibilities, ensuring participants leave with actionable insights they can apply immediately.

## Key Topics & Activities

1. Overview of Indonesia’s Personal Data Protection Law

* Understanding key articles, obligations, and implementation timelines.
* Exploring the business impact and compliance journey.

2. Hands-On Experience as a Data Steward

* Performing Data Discovery and Classification using IBM Knowledge Catalog.
* Identifying, cataloging, and protecting sensitive and personal data across the enterprise.

3. Hands-On Experience as a Data Privacy Officer by conducting essential assessments such as:

* Record of Processing Activity (RoPA)
* Privacy Assessments
* Data Protection Impact Assessments (DPIA)

4. Use Case Sharing and Best Practices

* Managing and responding to data breaches effectively.
* Automating data subject requests to meet regulatory deadlines.
* Implementing robust consent management frameworks to build trust with customers.

By the end of this workshop, participants will not only have a stronger understanding of Indonesia’s PDP Law but will also experience first-hand how IBM solutions can help operationalize compliance, reduce risks, and establish sustainable governance practices.
