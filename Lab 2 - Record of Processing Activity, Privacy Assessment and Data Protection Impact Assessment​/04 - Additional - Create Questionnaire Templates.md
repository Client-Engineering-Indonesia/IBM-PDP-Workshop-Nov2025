## Additional - Create Questionnaire Templates

To create additional template for example for internal assessment or Indonesia law that is not available in the GRC Portal. We can create custom Questionnaire Templates.

1. Go to hamburger menu on top left side -> expand **Assessments** -> **Questionnaire Templates**.
![alt text](https://github.com/Client-Engineering-Indonesia/IBM-PDP-workshop/blob/main/assets/img/op/22.png?raw=true)  
2. Click on **New** button to create new template.
3. Fill all the required fields and try to experiment with scoring method that are available.
![alt text](https://github.com/Client-Engineering-Indonesia/IBM-PDP-workshop/blob/main/assets/img/op/23.png?raw=true)
